About me

Hello! My name is Brandon Ford and this is my portfolio! I'm born and raised in the Northern Virginia area, which if you guessed it 100% explains the theme of this portfolio, but one of the great things about this area is the wildernest! It goes to pair with one of my favorite activities, hiking, so It's come to be a place I find myself whenever I need to relax or think. Some of my hobbies include gaming, car enthusiast, and as of late coding! I've come to love coding for its ability to problem solve. Problem solving and myself don't necessarily get along. In the sense of, whenever I'm presented with a problem I will not rest until it's solved, otherwise my mind will be leangering on it all day. And coding is problem solving, from an error wrong in the code to using functions to lessen some work, majority of the time we are solving a problem. That is the reason why I feel in love with coding.
